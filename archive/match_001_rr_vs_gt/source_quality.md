## Flagged Issues

1. **Unsourced situational context block (claims 18-24):** The entire "Situational Context" and "Travel & Fatigue" sections have zero source citations. These are likely verifiable from public fixture lists and points tables, but the News Analyst did not provide references. Low severity — claims are non-controversial — but violates sourcing discipline.

2. **"No fatigue concerns" (claim 25):** Stated as fact without any team or medical source. An 8-day gap and 6-day gap make fatigue unlikely, but "no concerns" is the analyst's conclusion, not a reported fact. Should be framed as "no fatigue concerns reported" or acknowledged as inference.

3. **Sooryavanshi impact sub prediction (claim 27):** The specific prediction ("likely comes in as substitute opener if RR chase") is unsourced. The "not confirmed" qualifier partially mitigates this, but "likely" still implies insider knowledge not demonstrated.

4. **Prasidh Krishna as GT impact sub (claim 30):** "Named as likely substitute" implies someone named him, but no source is given. Missing citation or speculative inference.

5. **Chasing win rate without sample size (claim 12):** "64-65% win rate" — all-time? This season? Last 3 seasons? Analytical value is limited without knowing the denominator.

6. **Pitch average score ambiguity (claim 9):** "Average first-innings score ~166-168" — time period not specified. May conflate different pitch eras.

7. **Spin claim is generic venue lore (claim 11):** "Spinners get help as match progresses" appears in every Sawai Mansingh preview. Not necessarily wrong but not specific to tonight's pitch preparation or curator's instructions.

8. **Sudharsan stats from GT's own site (claim 20):** Team's official site has incentive to present players in best light. Three successive fifties is verifiable but should be cross-checked.
