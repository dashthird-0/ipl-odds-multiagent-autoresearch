## Flagged Issues

1. **Cricket Addictor as a source (claims 10-11):** This is a fantasy cricket tips website, not a journalism outlet. Its pitch reports are commonly aggregated from other sources without attribution. Claims are partially rescued by Cricsheet corroboration but would be low-tier standing alone.

2. **Unsourced "55% field-first win rate" (claim 18):** This specific statistic appears in the Captaincy and Tactics section with no attribution. Could be derived from Cricsheet but is not labeled as such. Downstream agents should not treat this as a hard input.

3. **Small sample sizes insufficiently flagged (claims 11, 13):** "60% chasing wins in 2026" is based on 3 out of 5 matches. This is not statistically meaningful on its own and should not drive pricing decisions. The 2023-2026 sample (17/29) is more useful but still modest.

4. **Yahoo Sports date imprecision (claims 14, 23):** Two Yahoo Sports citations have only month-level dates ("2026-05"), making freshness verification harder. Both are within the current match cycle but precise publication timing is unknown.

5. **No pitch curator statement:** The report explicitly acknowledges this gap. All pitch characterization relies on captain toss comments and historical patterns. No direct curator statement about surface preparation exists in the evidence.

6. **Inglis fitness gap:** Pant confirmed selection at toss, but no physiotherapist or team management comment on percentage fitness. The gap between "selected" and "fully fit for sustained batting under pressure" is not addressed by any source.

7. **"CSK acclimatized as home team" (section 6):** Analyst inference stated without evidence of how long CSK have been in Chennai. They returned from Delhi (May 5) — a 5-day gap provides acclimatization time, but the claim is framed as more certain than warranted.

8. **Speculative framing in "Match-up note" (section 4):** The observation about Chepauk's spin-friendly nature giving CSK tactical edge is analyst judgment without a direct source, though it follows logically from the sourced Cricsheet spin data.
