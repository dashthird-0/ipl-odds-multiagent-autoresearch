1. **Resolving the market-vs-base-rate tension.** The market at 60.5% sits at the upper boundary of the band; the Skeptic's base-rate midpoint of ~54-55% sits near the center. I side predominantly with the Skeptic for these reasons: (a) PBKS's last-8 record of 4-4 is a more informative window than the full-season 6-3, given the 3-match losing streak is the most recent data; (b) PBKS's 6-8 record at Dharamsala directly contradicts home advantage; (c) DC's away form (3-2, 60%) is meaningfully better than their overall 36% season rate. The band center at ~55% is a compromise: 5.5pp below the market, 0.5pp above the Skeptic's midpoint. This reflects accepting most of the Skeptic's challenges while acknowledging the market may correctly price the dead-rubber and motivation asymmetry at slightly more than 2pp.
