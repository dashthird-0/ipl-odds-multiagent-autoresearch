**Whether SRH's 2026 squad composition makes the historical H2H irrelevant.** SRH's current batting lineup (Abhishek SR 188.5, Ishan SR 181.0, Klaasen SR 149.2) is genuinely exceptional and different from earlier SRH squads that GT dominated. If this squad-level step-change makes the 5-1 H2H a relic rather than a predictor, the model band should compress toward 50/50 or tilt slightly toward SRH. The market appears to have taken this view. The counter-argument is that GT's bowling attack (Rabada, Siraj, Rashid) is also elite and has consistently handled high-quality batting lineups at this venue.

## What Would Change This View

- **If SRH's powerplay aggression survives GT's pace attack (Abhishek and Ishan still batting after 6 overs):** Model band shifts toward SRH 52-55%, as the historical GT bowling advantage would be failing against the upgraded SRH batting.

- **If Travis Head enters as impact sub for the chase:** SRH's batting depth increases significantly for the second innings. This should raise SRH's implied probability by 1-2pp if it happens.
- **If dew materializes despite the low humidity forecast:** Would validate the market's toss-premium and shift the band toward SRH.
