## Pitch Report -- Shaheed Veer Narayan Singh International Stadium, Raipur

**Pitch type:** Black-soil wicket, plays on the **slower side** compared to Chinnaswamy.

**Key characteristics:**
- Slow and low surface with inconsistent bounce
- Offers good bounce and carry early (first 3-4 overs) then flattens out to become sluggish
- New-ball pacers may find lateral movement; spinners effective through middle overs
- Batters need time to settle -- powerplay execution is critical
- Boundaries: ~80m (larger than Chinnaswamy's compact dimensions)
- Par score: estimated 149-165 range

**Venue IPL stats (7 matches at Raipur):**
- Average 1st innings score: ~147-149 runs
- Highest team total: 167/8 (RCB vs MI, 2026)
- Lowest team total: 119/6 (CSK vs DD, 2015)
- **Chasing advantage: Teams batting second have won 5 of 7 matches (71.4%)**
- First innings batting SR: 138.61; Second innings batting SR: 143.48

Sources:
- Sportskeeda Raipur pitch history, ~May 12-13, 2026
- IPL.com venue analysis, ~May 12-13, 2026
- CricTracker preview, ~May 12-13, 2026

**Key implication:** This is NOT a Chinnaswamy-style run feast. The slower, lower surface with 80m boundaries and average 1st innings of ~149 means this is a significantly lower-scoring venue. Spinners (Narine, Varun, Suyash Sharma) become more important. The stats snapshot's venue splits for Chinnaswamy (avg 1st innings 188, chase wins 58.8%) should NOT be applied here.
