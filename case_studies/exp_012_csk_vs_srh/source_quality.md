## Flagged Issues

1. **Pitch characterisation rests on a single outlet (sportsyaari.com) with no curator backing.** Claims 14 and 15 describe pitch behaviour in vivid detail ("quicker now," "grass-laced surfaces," "multi-phase behavior") but this is journalist narrative from one source. There is no curator or groundstaff quote for this specific match. The analyst correctly flags this gap. All pitch behaviour claims beyond raw numbers should be treated as speculative.

2. **Rain probability sources conflict materially (10% vs 55%).** The sundayguardianlive.com article uses alarmist headline framing ("rain set to washout") while latestly.com gives a more measured 10-20% base with 50% risk for brief heavy spells. Neither appears to cite IMD directly by name. The cyclonic circulation detail adds credibility but the range remains wide. The Synthesizer should treat rain risk as genuinely uncertain (somewhere in the 15-50% range) rather than anchoring to either extreme.

3. **The sundayguardianlive.com weather article has clickbait framing.** The headline "rain set to washout today's IPL match" is sensationalist and not supported by the data in its own article. Its weather data readings are usable but its editorial framing should be ignored.

4. **Impact player claims are pure speculation.** Neither team's impact sub is known. Claims 6, 9 (impact part), and 11 are journalist guesswork. Travis Head entering as impact player for SRH would materially change SRH's batting strength, but this is not confirmed.

5. **sportsyaari.com par score (160-180) is in tension with its own 2026 average (189).** If the 2026 average first innings score at Chepauk is approximately 189, then a par score of 160-180 appears conservative by roughly 10-30 runs. This internal inconsistency suggests the par score may be recycled from older seasons or is based on all-time data (all-IPL avg 165), not current form.

6. **No curator/groundstaff quotes exist.** All pitch behaviour claims (Claims 14, 15, 16) rest entirely on journalist observation. Gaikwad's "dry surface" comment at the toss (Claim 20) is the closest thing to a first-hand pitch assessment and should be weighted above journalist pitch narratives.

7. **CSK qualification scenario sources are undated.** Claim 29 relies on sports.yahoo.com (undated) and crictracker.com (undated). The arithmetic is correct regardless of date, but the undated sourcing is noted.

8. **Wind data is from early morning, not match time.** The 23.8 km/h reading from businesstoday.in is described as an early morning weather station reading. Wind at 7:30 PM match time could differ materially.

9. **News24 prediction (Claim 10) is undated and now disproven.** It was included for completeness but has zero downstream value. Discard entirely.
